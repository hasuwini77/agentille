# Persona Walkthroughs

For 2 personas, simulate their first 10 seconds on the page. Note where attention goes, where it gets confused, where it drops off.

**Persona A — "Hurried Pro"** (busy senior dev, 3 tabs open, came from HN):
- Where does her eye go first?
- Can she answer "what is this?" in 5 seconds?
- Is the next action obvious?

**Persona B — "Curious Beginner"** (less-technical user, browsing on phone):
- Is the language understandable?
- Does the page assume knowledge they don't have?
- Where does the design lose them?
